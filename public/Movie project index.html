Rumble in the Bronx

One of the best movies ever made by Jackie Chan, it has everything parting from a inmigrant arriving to america then to be a social justice hero based on the circumstances then to play on the bad guys side because we are trying to build a better future for everyone, great movie, great message and great actors, not a prize award movie but definetely enterteining.
